About Me

I completed my Ph.D. from the Statistics Department at Columbia University, New York. I was jointly advised by Prof. Bodhisattva Sen and Prof. Sumit Mukherjee. I did my Bachelors and Masters in Statistics from Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata. Here is a copy of my CV.

I was the inaugural PIMS Kantorovich Initiative Postdoctoral Fellow at the University of British Columbia, Vancouver from 2022-23. I was jointly advised by Prof. Young-Heon Kim, Prof. Soumik Pal, and Prof. Geoffrey Schiebinger.

Starting July, 2023, I have joined the University of Chicago Booth School of Business as an Assistant Professor in the Econometrics and Statistics group.

Research

  • Nonparametric inference and testing

  • Optimal transport and measure transportation

  • Generative modeling via PDE/OT (e.g., Sinkhorn, mirror flows)

  • Inference for dependent data (Ising/graphical models)

  • U‑statistics, large deviations, and asymptotics

  • Causal inference
